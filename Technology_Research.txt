# Data Science and Data Analytics Research

## Introduction

Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, statistical techniques, programming, and machine learning to extract meaningful insights from data. Data Analytics focuses on analyzing data to support decision-making and improve business performance.

In today's digital world, organizations generate massive amounts of data. Data Science and Analytics help businesses understand trends, predict future outcomes, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## What is Data Science?

Data Science combines:

* Statistics
* Mathematics
* Programming
* Machine Learning
* Data Visualization
* Domain Knowledge

The primary goal of Data Science is to convert raw data into valuable insights that help organizations solve real-world problems.
